DTV Now


DTV Now is an AT&T over-the-top service that allows subscribers to stream their favorite programs from cable channels without a long-term commitment. As of November 2017, the service has 900,000 subscribers.

As a Sr UI Designer I worked alongside a team of UX/UI designers and developers on a design refresh across platforms that included their upcoming cloud-based DVR streaming service.
