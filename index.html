About me

I'm a product design lead with 11 years of experience building, shipping, and iterating digital products for platforms including iOS & Android, tvOS, web, television, and automotive. I have worked with A24, AT&T, Cablevision, Directv, Ford, Lionsgate, Microsoft, Mitsubishi, Sony, Southern California Edison, Universal, Warner Bros., and others creating immersive digital experiences that have a positive and lasting impact. You can see some examples of past projects on Behance and Dribbble, and connect with me on LinkedIn.

Team play, reliability communication and all-up positive vibes complete the experience working with [Jordan].
