ChirpChange allows you to donate a few cents to the charity of your choice every time Donald Trump tweets from his personal twitter account, @realDonaldTrump.

As a Designer/Front-end Developer, I worked closely with one of the founders—and Lead UX—of ChirpChange to create a fun and clean new look for the site and incorporate some new illustrations and animations.
