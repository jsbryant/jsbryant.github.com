Sony

PlayStation Store | Destiny


The PlayStation Store is a digital media store that offers a range of downloadable content that includes full games, add-on content, playable demos, themes and game/movie trailers.

As a UI Designer I worked closely with a small team of UX and motion designers to create an immersive e-commerce experience that exhibited the visuals and gameplay of Sony’s PlayStation games.
