Lionsgate

Media Asset Management Site


The Lionsgate Media Asset Management Site is an internal aggregate for all key art, video and audio promotional materials associated with Lionsgate programming.

Lionsgate’s internal sales representatives were having trouble differentiating between the content of their internal site. As a Digital Art Director I worked closely with the client to implement a design refresh of their site that utilized the brand’s color system to sort and categorize the sites content, making it more accessible to the sales team.
